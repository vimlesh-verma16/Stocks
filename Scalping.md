# Scalping Strategy Quick Guide

## 1-Minute Scalping
- Very fast execution and tight stops needed
- High-frequency trading, many trades daily
- High capital required for tiny price moves
- Very small targets, quick exits essential
- ⚠️ Highly risky - experts only

## 3-Minute Scalping
- Fast execution but more manageable
- Normal scalping, less intense than 1-min
- High capital needed, better profit potential
- Sufficient targets, flexible stop losses
- ⚠️ Moderate risk - intermediate traders

## 5-Minute Scalping (Best)
- Good execution speed, controlled approach
- Basic capital requirement (accessible)
- Big targets with wider profit zones
- Big stop losses allow breathing room
- ⚠️ Less risky - beginner-friendly, most balanced
