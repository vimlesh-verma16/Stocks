

# Scalping Strategy Quick Guide

Always be a maker on delta exchange.  
price should be lower then best price by 3 values.  
Market should come to your order. 
Important always apply Stops loss Crypto can move very quickly. 

- Make sure you always draw lines in for support and resistance
- Market always moves on supprt and resistance.
